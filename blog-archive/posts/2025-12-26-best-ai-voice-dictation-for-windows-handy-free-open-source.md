# Best AI Voice Dictation for Windows - Handy (Free & Open-Source)

If you use voice dictation regularly, you already know how frustrating it can be to find something that’s accurate, fast, private, and not locked behind a subscription, especially on Windows.

That’s exactly the problem I was trying to solve.

## Why I Needed an Alternative

I’m a Mac user, and on macOS I absolutely love **<a href="https://tryvoiceink.com/" class="ek-link" aria-label="Voice Ink (opens in a new tab)" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Voice Ink</a>**. It’s free and open-source (with a paid one-time purchase license for updates), works offline, and is extremely accurate. The only issue? **It’s not available on Windows.**

Since I also work on Windows, I went looking for a similar solution.

- "Win + H" the built-in dictation → hit or miss
- Not great if English isn’t your first language
- Accuracy drops fast in real-world usage

I briefly tried **<a href="https://wisprflow.ai/" class="ek-link" target="_blank" aria-label="Wispr Flow (opens in a new tab)" rel="noreferrer noopener">Wispr Flow</a>**, but even during the trial period, it didn’t feel accurate enough and the subscription model didn’t help.

## Enter Handy

I actually discovered <a href="https://handy.computer/" class="ek-link" aria-label="Handy (opens in a new tab)" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"><strong>Handy</strong></a> thanks to the developer of Voice Ink, Pax. And honestly? It took me less than a few minutes to uninstall Wispr Flow and switch completely.

Handy is:

- Free and open-source (<a href="https://github.com/cjpais/Handy" class="ek-link" target="_blank" aria-label="github (opens in a new tab)" rel="noreferrer noopener">github</a>)
- Fully offline (no internet required)
- Cross-platform (works perfectly on Windows)
- Simple, fast, and gets out of your way

It’s not as feature-rich as Voice Ink, but for Windows, it does exactly what I needed it to do.

If you already use dictation daily, or want to start, <a href="https://handy.computer/" class="ek-link" target="_blank" aria-label="Handy (opens in a new tab)" rel="noreferrer noopener">Handy</a> is worth installing immediately. Just a solid tool that works.
