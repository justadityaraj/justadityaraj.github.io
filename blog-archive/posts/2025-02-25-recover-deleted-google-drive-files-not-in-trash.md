# How to Recover Deleted Google Drive Files That Are Not in the Trash

I accidentally deleted important files from Google Drive and couldn't find them in the Trash, but Google Support was able to restore them for me. Here's how:

## Step 1: Contact Google Support

Visit one of these official support pages:

- <a href="https://support.google.com/drive/" class="ek-link">Google Drive Help</a> (scroll down and click Contact Us)
- <a href="https://support.google.com/googleone/gethelp" class="ek-link">Google One Support</a>

Choose the contact option: **Chat (preferred), Email,** or **Call**.

## Step 2: Request File Recovery

Once connected, explain that you've lost files that are no longer in Trash. The support agent will check if they can be restored using Google's recovery tool.

### Important Things to Know

Some files are restored immediately, while others may take 24-48 hours. For me, it took less than a minute to recover 5 GB of files.

**Files deleted from Trash more than 25 days ago** are permanently purged and **cannot be recovered.**

The sooner you contact Google, the better the chances of recovery!
